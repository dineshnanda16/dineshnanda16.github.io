WCare

A Health Platform for Women

Developed a web platform to support safe and healthy childbirth for pregnant women in India, especially in low-connectivity areas. The site includes voice commands, multilingual chatbot support using Langchain, access to government assistance, and offline services through IVR. Built using React,React Native,FireBase, and Twilio for robust performance and accessibility both in web aswell as app.
